About the Lab

The LACS Lab (Laboratory for Advanced Cryptography & Security) at IIT Patna is a research group dedicated to building the next generation of secure digital systems. The lab's work spans quantum-resistant cryptographic protocols, applied cybersecurity, blockchain and smart-contract security, and security for IoT and distributed networks. Scholars in the lab work on funded and independent projects, publish in leading journals and conferences, and collaborate with researchers across premier Indian institutions.
